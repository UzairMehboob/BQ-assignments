Aalmi Urdu Conference

  • About Conference

    • Aalmi Urdu Conference is a remarkable occasion that gathers intellectuals from around the world under one roof to celebrate the glory of our languages and literature. The conference celebrates not only the beauty of Urdu literature but also the literary treasure preserved in the many languages spoken in the country. Over the years, the conference has hosted several literary figures, artists, educationalists, and language experts in its scholarly and highly knowledgeable sessions. The conference's essence resonates with the Arts Council's aim to celebrate and promote our language, culture, and literature globally.
